The Demolitionist

Demolitionist is a mastery in Grim Dawn that focuses on deployment of incendiary, explosive, and otherwise harmful munitions. While Demolitionists excel at range, none of their skills are weapon-dependent, so both melee and ranged builds can be made viable.

Destruction.

Weapons and war machines used by Demolitionists

A deadly alchemical technique causes martial attacks to combust the air around the enemy.
A blinding light temporarily confuses enemies and disrupts their ability to defend themselves.
The Grenado is a recent invention that has quickly grown in use on the battlefield.
A mortar that will continuously lob explosives into the air until depleted.
